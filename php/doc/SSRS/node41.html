Product Functions

  1. Simplifying tasks to leaders of groups, such as:
    1. Sending notifications to group members, prospective members, former members, and interested community members (email)
    2. Sending information (files) to group members via email or download link
    3. Managing a group-wide calendar of events
      1. track volunteers, attendees, and contributors
      2. suggest potentially beneficial services other groups could provide related to the event
      3. provide a calendar of events that includes events from other groups that members would want to attend (like marching band if half of LCM are in the marching band.)
      4. keeping track of who is responsible for bringing / doing what at an event
    4. Automatically generating:
      1. Contact information (contact sheets, phone directories)
      2. Website with updated contact, group, event, and customized information
      3. Organization charts
      4. Graphical relationships between groups
      5. Fees, dues, and expenses notifications
      6. Event reminders
  2. Consolidating information for members, former members, potential members (and parents) of groups:
    1. Common location of group information
    2. Searching existing groups
    3. Tying together existing groups (even suggesting similar groups)
    4. Personalized emails regarding changes/updates
    5. Outstanding expenses or reimbursements
    6. Reliable (i.e., automatically updated):
      1. Group contact information
      2. Group event information
    7. Transcript of verified group activity (for use with service-learning classes, and proof of volunteerism for potential employers)
    8. Supplementing Vandal Friday with emails to prospective high school Seniors
  3. Getting member input though: forums, project managers, surveys, and polls
  4. Payment processing and sponsorship collection
  5. 4. Recruitment and advertising for groups, volunteer / paid opportunities, services provided, possibly a bartering tool
