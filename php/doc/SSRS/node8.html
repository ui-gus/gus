Scope

GUS is to social-networking as an intranet is to the Internet. Where other social networks distract users with non-university non-local non-face-to-face non-involvement, GUS will focus these types of functionalities to the university and local community setting to best meet the administrative and service needs of groups in a university setting. In addition to being a group-centered student-involvement web-application.
