Identification

The software system being considered for development is referred to as Groups in a University Setting or gus. The customer providing specifications for the ethnic and religion team is the Lutheran Campus Ministry. The ultimate customer, or end-user, of the system will be student groups at the University of Idaho. This is a new project effort, so the version under development is version 0.05
